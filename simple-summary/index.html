My first bag of Blue Bottle Coffee!

Yesterday evening I got my first order from Blue Bottle Coffee Roasters! I chose their “House of Good” blend. It’s definitely an espresso blend, and to my taste buds I get hints of blackberry and almonds. I chose to try my first cup from a Chemex and I thoroughly enjoyed it.
