tr.Paper

tr.Paper draws glowing circuit-like traces with crisp, uniform angles. Tweak settings, export wallpaper, or export the code with your current settings baked in.

Export Types

  • Normal: Readable IIFE with settings baked in. Good for tweaking.
  • Slim (Raw): Minified source with line breaks. Good for embedding.
  • Slim (Eval): Single-line eval(atob(...)) for fast drop-in.
